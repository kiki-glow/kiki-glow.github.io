About Us

Glow Techs is a private corporation aimed at Graphics designing. It was launched in November 2022, with its roots in Kenya but operating globally.

I specialize extensively at UX Designing which involves the design of the entire process of acquiring and integrating the product, including aspects of branding, design, usability and function, but also other areas of Graphics Designing. Here, you will find all the info you need about Graphics Designing. In case of any queries, please contact me via any of my socials. Thank you!
